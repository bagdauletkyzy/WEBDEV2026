Pasta Chicken

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 200g
Chicken 250g
Garlic 2 cloves
cream 150 ml

Step 1: Cook pasta until al dente, reserve ½ cup of pasta water.

Step 2: Sauté garlic in olive oil, add chicken strips, season, and cook until golden; stir in cream and simmer.

Step 3: Combine pasta with sauce, add pasta water for creaminess, and finish with parmesan or herbs.

Avocado Toast with Egg

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
bread 2 slices
avocado 1 ripe
egg 1

Step 1: Toast the bread until golden.

Step 2: Mash avocado with a pinch of salt and spread on toast.

Step 3: Fry or poach the egg, place on top, sprinkle with pepper.
